Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

At EduraZ, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service. We're committed to transparency and keeping your data safe.

By using EduraZ, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, we're here to help.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use EduraZ to generate a learning roadmap, you share:

  • Your learning goals and what you want to achieve
  • Your preferred timeframe and daily schedule
  • Your experience level and learning style preferences

This information helps us create personalized roadmaps tailored to your needs.

Automatically Collected Information

To improve our service, we automatically collect basic usage information:

  • Device and browser information
  • How you interact with our website
  • General usage patterns (anonymized)

We use this data to understand how EduraZ is used and to make it better for everyone.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create your roadmap: Your goals and preferences power the AI that generates your personalized learning plan
  • Improve EduraZ: We analyze usage patterns to make the service faster, smarter, and more helpful
  • Keep you safe: We monitor for security issues and protect against abuse

We never sell your data. We never use it for advertising. Your information exists solely to make EduraZ work better for you.

Data Storage and Security

We take security seriously. Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption and security practices. We store only what's necessary to provide you with roadmaps and improve the service.

As an MVP, we keep data collection minimal. We don't store sensitive personal information unless it's essential for EduraZ to function. Your learning goals are yours—we're just here to help you achieve them.

Third-Party Services

EduraZ may use third-party services for analytics and functionality. These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to processing of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

EduraZ is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Concerns about your data? We're here to help. Reach out through our website, and we'll get back to you promptly.

This policy is effective as of December 2024 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.
