Security

Your data security is our priority

Our Commitment

Your data security isn't optional. It's essential. At EduraZ, we're committed to protecting your information with industry-leading security practices. When you use our service, you can trust that your data is safe.

Security Measures

We protect your data with multiple layers of security:

HTTPS Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.

Minimal Data Collection

We only collect the information necessary to provide our services. We don't store sensitive personal data unnecessarily.

Secure Infrastructure

Our services are hosted on secure, reputable platforms with regular security updates and monitoring.

Regular Updates

We continuously update our systems and practices to address emerging security threats and vulnerabilities.

Data Protection

What We Protect

We protect all information you provide when using EduraZ, including:

  • Learning goals and preferences
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Any information you choose to share

How We Protect It

We use a combination of technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest where applicable
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Secure coding practices

Third-Party Services

EduraZ may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, and functionality. We carefully select partners who maintain high security standards and comply with relevant data protection regulations.

Reporting Security Issues

Found a security issue? We want to know. Please report any vulnerabilities or concerns immediately. We take every report seriously and will investigate promptly.

For everyone's safety, please give us time to fix issues before making them public. We appreciate your help in keeping EduraZ secure.

Your Role in Security

Security is a partnership. Here's how you can help keep your data safe:

  • Keep software updated: Regular updates patch security vulnerabilities
  • Use strong passwords: If you create an account, choose a unique, strong password
  • Be mindful: Only share information you're comfortable with
  • Report issues: If something seems off, let us know immediately

Updates to Security Practices

As threats evolve, so do our security practices. We continuously improve our security measures and will update this page as our practices change.

This security information is current as of December 2024.
